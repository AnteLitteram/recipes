No bake chocolate peanut butter oatmeal cookies

2 cups sugar
1 stick butter
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup coco
3 cups oatmeal
1 cup peanut butter
1 teaspoon vinilla extract
1 pinch sale

Bring sugar, butter, milk, and coco to a boil.

Let boil for 1 min

Remove from heat.

Add peanut butter, oatmeal, salt, and vanilla.

Mix throughly.

Spoon out bite sizes with teaspoon.
